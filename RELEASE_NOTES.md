# Release Notes

Version v0.1.0 — June 26, 2026

First public build of AxiRoster — a focused WvW guild roster tool for guild
leadership.

## One roster from every source
Pull your GW2 guild members and your Discord roster (via AxiTools), and AxiRoster
folds them into a single row per person — in-game accounts, Discord members,
manual links, and auto-links reconciled together. Add nicknames, tags, notes, and
manual Discord↔GW2 links, and overlay WvW activity (main class, class spread, raid
attendance, time in raids, last seen) from AxiBridge reports.

## Shared sync — sign in with Discord, no setup
Leadership can now share one live workspace without anyone standing up a database.
Sign in with Discord, and a guild **leader** claims the guild (verified by their
GW2 leader key); from then on tags, notes, links, and the roster sync live across
officers. Owners invite officers by Discord username or a shareable code, and
invitees accept or reject from their own app. Access is **read** or **write**, and
only the owner manages members and the key.

NOTE: Own more than one guild? Claim each one — the workspace follows your active
guild, so every guild has its own members, invites, and roster.

## Built to stay current
AxiRoster auto-updates: when a new version ships you'll see it download in the
titlebar and can restart to install. Windows, macOS (signed + notarized), and
Linux builds are published on every release.
